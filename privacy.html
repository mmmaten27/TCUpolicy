Privacy Policy for Nexus Post

Last updated: April 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Nexus Post collects, uses, and protects your information in accordance with Meta Platform Policies.

1. Data Collection

We only collect data necessary for the application's core functionality, including:

  • Public Profile: Name and profile picture from your Facebook account.
  • Access Tokens: Temporary credentials used to authorize API requests for posting.
  • Social Assets: A list of Facebook Pages and Instagram/Threads accounts that you have authorized for the app to manage.

2. Use of Data

The data collected is used strictly to enable Nexus Post to publish, schedule, and manage content on your behalf. We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal data or access tokens to any third parties.

3. Data Security

All Access Tokens are stored securely using industry-standard encryption (AES-256) to ensure they are protected from unauthorized access.

4. User Data Deletion

Users have the right to request the deletion of their data at any time. You can do so through:

  • Facebook Settings: Navigate to Settings & Privacy > Apps and Websites, find "Nexus Post", and click "Remove".
  • Direct Request: Send a deletion request to the developer via the contact email below. We will permanently delete your data from our servers within 24 hours.
